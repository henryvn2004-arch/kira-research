Why we built KIRA

Good market research has always been expensive — $20,000 for a custom study, weeks of turnaround, and output that's often outdated before it's delivered. For most organizations entering Southeast Asian markets, that's prohibitive.

We built KIRA RESEARCH to change that. By combining the analytical rigor of a proprietary library of 1,000+ primary market studies with real-time web intelligence and Claude AI's synthesis capabilities, we can deliver consulting-grade reports in minutes at a fraction of the traditional cost.

Every report is grounded in how these markets actually work — the channel structures, margin dynamics, consumer behaviors, and regulatory nuances that took years to document in the field. The AI doesn't guess. It synthesizes.

How our intelligence works

🔍
Live Research
Claude AI runs real-time web research, pulling current market data, competitor intelligence, regulatory updates, and industry news at the moment of generation.
📚
Research Library RAG
A vector search engine pulls the most relevant frameworks and patterns from our proprietary library of primary market studies — structural insights that web search can't surface.
✍️
AI Synthesis
Claude Sonnet combines live data with historical patterns to write each section sequentially — building context as it goes, the way a human analyst would.
💬
Interactive Follow-up
Every report includes an AI chatbox trained on your specific report. Ask follow-up questions, drill into sections, or explore strategic implications in real time.

What we stand for

Analytical rigor

We don't produce summaries of things you could Google. Every report applies a structured analytical framework, separates signal from noise, and draws explicit conclusions.

Grounded in primary research

Our knowledge base is built on field research — consumer surveys, trade interviews, channel audits. That institutional knowledge underpins every AI-generated report.

Transparent limitations

AI-generated analysis has limits. We're upfront about what we can and can't verify, and our chatbox is designed to acknowledge uncertainty rather than fabricate confidence.

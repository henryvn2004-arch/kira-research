How it works

One topic in. A finished report out.

No prompt engineering. No setup. Describe what you want; our engine handles the rest.

Describe the topic.

A sentence is enough. "EV market in South Korea 2027 — demand outlook, competitive landscape, policy environment." Country, sector, year, angle — whatever matters.

Optional: attach up to 5 docs (PDF, DOCX, XLSX, CSV) to ground the research in your data.

Our engine plans the structure.

Topic gets parsed — country, sector, language, intent. A section structure gets drafted (~10 sections: macro, sector overview, competitive landscape, demand, regulatory, outlook).

Uses the same 7-stage workflow as our internal consulting pipeline.

Research runs in two languages.

Web search fires queries in English plus the country's business-press language — Vietnamese for Vietnam, Korean for Korea, Portuguese for Brazil. Local stats bureaus, industry associations, central bank filings.

Source aliases stay English in the report; original-language source names captured in the page-bottom source key.

You get a PDF + the web edition.

Sections drafted, charts placed (where data warrants), pages rendered. ~10 minutes total. Saved to your private library — open in browser, download the PDF, or archive when done.

Visually identical to our published consulting reports. No watermarks, no "AI-generated" disclaimers.

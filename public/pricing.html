Frequently asked questions
How is KIRA RESEARCH different from a Google search?
KIRA doesn't surface links — it synthesizes. Every report combines live web research with patterns extracted from a proprietary library of 1,000+ primary market studies. The output is structured, analytical, consulting-grade prose, not a list of articles to read.
How current is the data?
Each report runs live web research at generation time, pulling current market data, news, and competitive intelligence. The structural frameworks come from our historical research library and are clearly separated from current data.
How long does generation take?
Competitive Analysis takes ~2 minutes. Market Entry Briefs take ~3 minutes. Industry Deep Dives take 4–6 minutes depending on the complexity of the market. You can leave the page — your report is saved and accessible from your profile.
Can I access my report again after closing the browser?
Yes. Every report is saved to your account permanently. Log in and visit your profile to access all past reports at any time. The AI chatbox remains active too.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit and debit cards via PayPal — no PayPal account required. Transactions are processed securely by PayPal.
Do you offer refunds?
If your report fails to generate due to a technical issue on our side, we will refund in full or regenerate at no charge. As reports are generated on-demand using AI, we cannot offer refunds for completed reports.

What you should know before buying.

How is payment handled?
PayPal at checkout, billed in USD. The PayPal flow accepts credit cards as well — no PayPal account required.
What if the report doesn't match what I expected?
Every report has a free preview section so you can read before buying. If the published report materially misses what the preview promised, write to hello@kiraresearch.com and we'll make it right.
Can I share the PDF with my team?
The standard license is single-user. Need to share internally? Email us about a team license at the same per-seat economics.
How often are library reports refreshed?
Each report carries a last-refresh date. Major changes in the market trigger a refresh. Buyers get those updates free for 12 months from purchase.
Can I buy the same report in multiple languages?
Yes. Each locale (EN / JA / KO) is sold separately at $39 — same content, native-language edition with reviewed translation.
How do I commission custom research?
Send us the question on the Custom Research page. We'll scope it, propose a deliverable and timeline, and confirm pricing before you commit.

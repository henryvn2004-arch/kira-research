ENGAGEMENT MODEL

From brief to delivery in four steps.

Every custom engagement runs through the same review-led process. No outsourced analyst pools, no ghost authors — your project is led end-to-end by a senior analyst on our team.

01 · DISCOVERY
Scoping call
30-minute call to understand the decision behind the brief, current evidence base, and where the answer matters most.
02 · DESIGN
Research plan
Written scope: research questions, methodology, sources, expert-interview targets, deliverable shape, timeline. Sign-off before fieldwork.
03 · FIELDWORK
Primary research
In-region interviews, desk research, data analysis. Modern tools (including AI for synthesis) accelerate the work; senior analysts own the judgment.
04 · DELIVERY
Report & working session
Final report, structured findings, and a working session to walk through implications. One revision round included.
